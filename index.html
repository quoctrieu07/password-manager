Crypto Specifications

All encrypted vaults undergo secure PBKDF2 iterations (100,000 passes, SHA-256) to derive a rugged 256-bit symmetric encryption key. We encrypt your entire groups, entries schema using authenticated AES-GCM (Galois/Counter Mode) preventing structure tampering, all safely on your own client sandbox.
